// THE STORY

Two careers, one obsession: turning discipline into something that looks effortless.

I'm Sándor, born in Hungary, now living on the Côte d'Azur. For years I was a ballet dancer — a craft built on relentless repetition, where the goal is to make the impossible look weightless. That training never left me; it just found new stages.

Today I'm an AI-native indie developer and a photographer. I build games and apps on Linux and for Apple platforms with SwiftUI, write small Python tools for my own work, and shoot the Riviera on older digital cameras. AI lets one person build at a scale that used to take a studio — but the taste, the structure, and the vision stay human.

I'm not chasing the crowd or the hype cycle. I'd rather make a few honest things with care, and ship them native — close to the metal, close to the truth.
